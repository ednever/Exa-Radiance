Exa Radiance

Version:

Company: Alpha CRC

About: Exa Radiance is there to help our users to highlight every desired word on any desired webpage!

Exa highlights words from user-created lists.
Lists could be filled manually or through existing files (Google Sheets, etc.)

Exa also contains all the basic functionality that a user might need: Screenshots, Status assignment to any highlighted word, and many other features.
